Strawberry Eton Mess

Make this practically instant dessert for a sweet treat for spring or summer months.

Here i'll show you how to prepare a delicious Strawberry Eton Mess

ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 pint strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon strawberry jam
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ounces store-bought meringues (broken into pieces, totaling roughly 3 cups)

Steps

  1. Toast the almonds
  2. Crush half the strawberries with the jam
  3. Whip the cream, but not too firmly
  4. Assemble the Eton mess and serve
